# Dog Care Guide

Proper care ensures a healthy, happy dog.

## Nutrition

- Feed a balanced diet according to breed, age, and activity.
- Fresh water should always be available.
- Avoid toxic foods:
  - Chocolate
  - Grapes and raisins
  - Onions and garlic

- Schedule **annual vet visits**.
- Vaccinations and parasite prevention.
- Watch for signs of illness:
  - Loss of appetite
  - Lethargy
  - Unusual behavior
