# Popular Dog Breeds

Dogs come in many shapes and sizes. Here are some of the most beloved breeds:

## Labrador Retriever

- Friendly, intelligent, and outgoing
- Excellent family dogs and therapy dogs
- Weight: 55–80 lbs
- Lifespan: 10–12 years

## German Shepherd

- Loyal, courageous, and versatile
- Frequently used in police and service work
- Weight: 50–90 lbs
- Lifespan: 9–13 years

## Golden Retriever

- Intelligent, friendly, and devoted
- Great with children and other pets
- Weight: 55–75 lbs
- Lifespan: 10–12 years

### Choosing the Right Breed

- Consider your **living situation**: apartment vs house
- Evaluate **activity level**
- Consider **allergies** and grooming needs
- Think about **temperament with children and other pets**
