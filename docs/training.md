# Dog Training Basics

Training strengthens the bond between you and your dog. Consistency and patience are key.

## Positive Reinforcement

- Reward good behavior immediately.
- Use treats, praise, or play.
- Avoid punishment.

Example:
Owner: "Sit!"
Dog sits
Owner: Gives treat + verbal praise

## Basic Commands

1. Sit
2. Stay
3. Come
4. Leave it
5. Heel

## House Training

- Take your dog outside frequently.
- Praise immediately after elimination.
- Establish a routine.
